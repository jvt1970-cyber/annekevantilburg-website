Open-air Gatherings

Female Farm Dances

What it is

Dance barefoot.
Under open sky.

Female Farm Dances is an open-air ecstatic dance gathering held on a farm or in a natural setting, a celebration of movement, freedom, and wild collective joy.

These events are born from a simple belief: that the body remembers how to dance, and that nature holds a particular kind of permission to let go. When bare feet meet the earth and music fills the air, something ancient stirs.

Rooted in the tradition of ecstatic dance, sober, conscious, judgment-free, these gatherings are open to all genders and all levels of movement experience. You don't need to know how to dance. You just need to show up.

The experience

What a Farm Dance feels like

  • 🌿

    Arrive in nature

    Fields, trees, sky. A setting that invites the nervous system to relax before a single beat has dropped.

  • 🎶

    Move to the music

    Anneke guides the energy with a curated 3–4 hour journey, building, peaking, and gently landing.

  • 🤝

    Find your community

    No phones on the dance floor. Eye contact welcome. A shared container of trust and acceptance.

  • 🌙

    Rest and integrate

    Closing circle, silence, tea. Space to land back in the body and carry the feeling home.
