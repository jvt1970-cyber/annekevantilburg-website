Embodied Coaching

Her approach

The body holds
the answers.

Most coaching begins in the mind: in strategies, plans, and reframing. Anneke's coaching begins in the body.

She believes that the nervous system, the breath, and the physical sensations we carry hold the most honest information we have about ourselves. Before we can think our way forward, we need to feel our way home.

Her sessions combine somatic awareness, honest dialogue, and creative exploration. They are not therapy; they are a space for the person you are becoming to emerge, clearly and without apology.
