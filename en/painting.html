Intuitive Painting

About the workshops

Pick up a brush.
Let go of the rules.

Anneke's intuitive painting workshops are not about learning to paint. They are about unlearning the idea that you can't.

These sessions are an invitation to make a mess, to be surprised by colour, and to discover what happens when you stop thinking and start feeling. No prior experience needed; in fact, it often gets in the way.

Each workshop includes guided warm-ups, music, movement, and plenty of paint. You will leave with something on canvas, and something shifted inside.

What to expect

A day in the workshop

1

Arrive & settle

Tea, connection, and a gentle warm-up to arrive fully in your body before touching a brush.

2

Move & feel

A short movement practice to drop out of the head and into sensation: your palette begins here.

3

Paint freely

Canvas, music, and the freedom to create without rules. Anneke moves through the space as a guide, not a teacher.

4

Share & close

A gentle closing circle to share what came up. Not about the painting; about the process.
