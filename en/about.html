Her Story

About Anneke

Who she is

A creative who lives between worlds

Anneke van Tilburg grew up with a paintbrush in one hand and music in her ears. From an early age she understood that the deepest truths don't always arrive in words — sometimes they come in colour, in rhythm, in the way the body knows something before the mind catches up.

After years of working in the creative field, she found her way into ecstatic dance — first as a dancer, then as a DJ. She discovered the power of music to unlock the body and dissolve the stories we carry. This realisation changed everything.

Today, Anneke weaves together three distinct practices: intuitive painting workshops, ecstatic dance DJ sets, and embodied coaching. Each one is, at its heart, an invitation — to feel more, to move freely, to create without fear.

Behind the scenes

In the studio & behind the decks

Her philosophy

Creativity is not a talent. It's a birthright.

Anneke believes that every human being is creative by nature — and that the conditioning we receive, the inner critic we develop, slowly buries this truth. Her work is about remembering.

Whether through a brush, through music, or through honest conversation — she holds a space where people can stop performing and start being.

Feel first

The body knows before the mind does. Her work always begins in sensation, not concept.

No rules, no wrong

In Anneke's spaces, there is no right way to dance, paint, or grow. Only your way.

Community over audience

She creates for the collective — her events are not performances, they are gatherings.

The Journey

How it all unfolded

2008

First brush stroke

Anneke picks up painting as a way to process emotion. What begins as a hobby quickly becomes a spiritual practice.

2014

First ecstatic dance

She attends her first ecstatic dance event and recognises something she had been missing — movement without rules, music without performance.

2017

Behind the decks

After years as a dancer, Anneke steps into the DJ booth and starts creating sound journeys for ecstatic dance events across the Netherlands.

2019

First painting workshop

She starts offering intuitive painting workshops — spaces without rules, where the inner critic is not welcome.

2021

Female Farm Dances

Anneke launches Female Farm Dances — a series of outdoor dance gatherings rooted in nature, community, and feminine wisdom.

Now

Three worlds, one invitation

Painting, dance, and coaching weave together into a full offering — each practice a doorway back to the self.
