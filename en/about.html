Her Story

About Anneke

Who she is

A creative with a passion for change

I work with three forms of expression: coaching, music, and painting. Mostly separately, each with its own entry point. What they share is a direction: beneath the surface, towards what is stuck, what has been forgotten, what wants to flow again. My compass is simple: from sur-viving to living, with joy!

Since 1998 I have coached people who feel something is ready to shift, but aren't yet sure what or how. I don't work with fixed methods; I work with what is here right now. Sometimes that is language, sometimes silence. But mostly the body, finally allowed to be honest.

Six years ago I rediscovered painting: a language beyond words. Paint and image bring me back to intuition, play, and freedom, discovering by following what wants to reveal itself.

In music too I choose that same honesty. At 55 I stepped behind the turntables to follow a dream. I'm not one for effects and tricks; I prefer to share music as purely as possible, so you can truly feel it. My style is intuitive and eclectic: Latin, soul, tropical rhythms, funk, reggae, (afro)house, medicine music, crossover and world music. An invitation to move, to feel, and to connect.

Behind the scenes

In the studio & behind the decks

My philosophy

A track is a work of art.

Months of work, of searching, of feeling, cast into sound. Who am I to think I can improve it? I let music be what it is. No tricks, no masks.

It is precisely in that honesty that tracks can complement each other, create contrast, or offer breathing room: that liminal space where you pause for a moment before the next thing carries you away.

Being present

I DJ sometimes standing, sometimes dancing, sometimes sitting. My body has a say. I find that difficult, but I am learning to embrace it.

It's not about me

It's about the dance, the dancers, the music. Not effects or tricks; connection.

My name is Anneke

The most pure form of myself there is. Let us meet on the dance floor, free from all must, exactly as we are.

The Journey

How it all unfolded

2008

First brush stroke

Anneke picks up painting as a way to process emotion. What begins as a hobby quickly becomes a spiritual practice.

2014

First ecstatic dance

She attends her first ecstatic dance event and recognises something she had been missing: movement without rules, music without performance.

2017

Behind the decks

After years as a dancer, Anneke steps into the DJ booth and starts creating sound journeys for ecstatic dance events across the Netherlands.

2019

First painting workshop

She starts offering intuitive painting workshops: spaces without rules, where the inner critic is not welcome.

2021

Female Farm Dances

Anneke launches Female Farm Dances: a series of outdoor dance gatherings rooted in nature, community, and feminine wisdom.

Now

Three worlds, one invitation

Painting, dance, and coaching weave together into a full offering, each practice a doorway back to the self.
