Anneke as DJ

Music that moves
more than the body

Anneke is not just a DJ; she is a curator of feeling. Her sets are carefully woven journeys: building slowly, diving deep, and always returning the dancer to a place of openness and aliveness.

Drawing from world music, organic beats, tribal rhythms, and conscious electronic sounds, she creates a musical arc that supports the body in letting go of whatever it is carrying.

She has played at ecstatic dance events, conscious festivals, retreat spaces, and private ceremonies across the Netherlands and beyond.
